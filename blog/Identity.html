Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.

I identify as an Indian who was born in Kenya and moved to England at the age of two. My culture and upbringing has been central to who I am, where I come from and the values that have been instilled in me. My family moved to England from Kenya following the end of colonisation. I grew up in a mainly traditional Indian home with three siblings and grandparents. As children our caregivers were mainly our grandparents as both parents worked long hours so they could establish a new life. My core values come from my grandparents who taught us to be grateful, to be respectful and to be honest. These values were taught using traditional folktales that always had a moral that they would share. These stories and values have been passed to my own children.

    My core values are:
  • respect
  • integrity and trust
  • beomg grateful
